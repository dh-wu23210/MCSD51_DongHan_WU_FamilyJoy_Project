# Sprint 5 PRD - Overview

## 1. Scope
Sprint 5 delivers the minimal Wish loop using backpack items.

Included
- Child can make a wish from a backpack item (consumes quantity immediately).
- Parent can accept a child wish with confirmation.
- Wish disappears on both sides after acceptance.
- Notifications are sent to parent and child on acceptance.

Deferred
- Wish descriptions by child.
- Wish tree health, fruit trays, essence system.

## 2. Notes
- Button label: `Make Wish` (child) and `Accept` (parent).
